SERVICES

If you want to commission us, read below for more information.

For Illustrations

What to Expect

The final piece will be unwatermarked and watermarked with simple background and decorative elements. Our schedule can be hectic, and it can affect the average duration of how long we work, we will inform you via email if ever that event occur. You'll receive a high resolution PNG file (within ranges 3500-3500 pixels). We email updates about the progress so be sure to check your emails because we need your feedback in case you've decided to have a couple of changes.

Here is the list of things we cannot and won't do:

  • Gore
  • Machinery
  • Furries
  • Violence
  • Armor

Terms of Services

  • Process of payments will be discussed via email.
  • Payment must be sent in advance before we begin. It can be half or fully paid.
  • No cancellations in the midst of progress. Refunds are acceptable if the commission has not begun yet.
  • Do not claim it as your own. Must put credit.
  • You are not allowed to add your own watermark or post the one without our watermark.
  • Profitable commissions will be further discussed via email.
  • Unless stated otherwise (for private reasons), we have the right to publish the artwork to our portfolio and social medias.
  • Deadlines are acceptable but do not rush us. Tight deadlines will have additional charges.
  • We reserve the right to decline a commission if we am not able to do it.
  • Pricing will change depending on the difficulty.
